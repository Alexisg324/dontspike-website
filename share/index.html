Share Your Story

Your voice can protect someone else.

We're collecting stories from people who have been affected by drink spiking — as survivors, witnesses, or loved ones — for educational purposes. Your story, shared on your terms, can help others recognize the signs and take action.

All submissions are voluntary and confidential. You choose how much to share and whether to remain anonymous. Together, we amplify what's too often silenced.
