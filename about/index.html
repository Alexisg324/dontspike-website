Who We Are

A community built on safety, awareness & solidarity.

Don't Spike was founded to tackle one of the most underreported dangers in social settings — drink spiking. We believe that awareness is the first line of defense. Through education, storytelling, and community action, we're working to make every bar, club, festival, and gathering safer.

We are not just a brand — we're a movement. Every post, every shared story, and every conversation is a step toward a world where no one has to worry about what's in their drink.

Don't Spike has developed a patented detection straw designed to help people identify whether their drink has been tampered with — an accessible, everyday solution to a deeply serious public safety issue.

Join the Movement

"Your safety is non-negotiable. Watch your drink. Watch your friends."

Drink spiking affects people of all genders, ages, and backgrounds. It's a crime — and together, we can prevent it. Don't Spike is here to ensure no one faces it alone.

Our Mission

Making every night out safer.

We exist because drink spiking is real, widespread, and vastly underreported. Our goal is to change that — through awareness campaigns, community education, and innovative safety technology.
